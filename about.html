I am Lewis Eze, the founder of GenX WebDevs, a company I created to empower teenagers by providing them with opportunities to develop and showcase their web development skills. My passion for technology and a strong belief in youth empowerment have driven me to bridge the digital skills gap among teens and foster a new generation of tech-savvy leaders.

My journey began as a high school student at Parow High School, where I noticed the untapped potential in my peers and the lack of opportunities for them to explore careers in technology. This realization inspired me to start GenX WebDevs, a company that not only creates websites but also serves as a platform for teenagers to gain hands-on experience and build their portfolios.

In addition to running my company, I've led various initiatives, including organizing a coding competition at my school to ignite a passion for programming among students. I also played a key role in putting together the "Leadership Empowerment Day," which showcases my commitment to youth development.

My innovative approach to education and technology has gained attention and support from my community and local businesses. I am currently focused on expanding GenX WebDevs to reach more students and offer comprehensive training in web development, coding, and digital entrepreneurship.

As a forward-thinking leader, I am not only focused on building my company but also on creating a lasting impact on the next generation. I am determined to continue advocating for youth empowerment through technology, ensuring that young people are equipped with the skills they need to thrive in the digital age.
